Statements and Objectives

Mission Statement:

We are in the business of classified advertisements website including categories such as For Sale, Housing, and Jobs. Here people can find and offer items and services.

Objectives Statements

  • 1. Focus on user experience and satisfaction

  • 2. Provide a service based on trust and simplicity

  • 3. Offer local products and services

  • 4. Free and convenient for most users

  • 5. No bloatware on the classifies

  • 6. Provide value for the users

  • 7. Network and users first
