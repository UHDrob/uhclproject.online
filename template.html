Statements and Objectives

Mission Statement:

We are in the business of classified advertisements website with categories including for sale, items wanted, and community events.

Objectives Statements

  • 1. Focus on user satisfaction and experience

  • 2. Provide a serice based on trust and simplicity

  • 3. Do not focus on revenue or profits

  • 4. Offer local products and events

  • 5. Free and convenient for most users

  • 6. No bloatware on the classifies

  • 7. Provide value for the users

  • 8. Network and users first
